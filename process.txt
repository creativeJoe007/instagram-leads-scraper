Instagram Leads Scraper

A tool that allows marketers extract email and phone numbers, followers, following, post-total, profile-links and website links from instagram profiles

* We start by open an initial page just to have access to the search box
* We type the inputed query provided to us
* We extract the entire search results
* Filter out hashtags and leave just pages
* We open each individual profiles 
* Extract their profile, name, followers and following, profile-links and also web links
* Email/Mobile is compulsory, we don't extract pages without emails or phone number
